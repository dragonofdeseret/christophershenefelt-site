Christopher Shenefelt

Christopher Shenefelt is an artist and writer from Northern Utah. His work includes oil paintings and charcoal sketches exploring landscapes, symbolic imagery, and contemplative motifs influenced by religious philosophy and mystical traditions.

Raised in the Mormon tradition, he later studied a wide range of religious and philosophical traditions including Eastern Orthodoxy, Catholicism, Protestant theology, Islam, and Daoism. His writing focuses on theology and comparative philosophy.

Since 2018 he has practiced Zazen meditation and Douglas Harding’s “Headless Way,” both of which influence his artistic approach.
