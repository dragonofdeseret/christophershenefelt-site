Bio

Christopher Shenefelt is an artist and writer based in Northern Utah.

His work explores symbolism, mysticism, and comparative philosophy through oil painting, charcoal sketches, and theological writing.

Influences include Van Gogh, Monet, Seurat, Klimt, and Escher, along with traditions from Daoism, Christianity, and contemplative practice.
